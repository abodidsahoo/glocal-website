A British film production house with India at its heart. We bring a global production sensibility to stories that are deeply local in their roots, in the belief that the most culturally specific cinema carries the greatest universal power.

The Founders

The Visionaries

Producer · Glocal Films UK Limited

Partha Sarathi Panda

Originally from Jagatsinghpur, Odisha, and now based in London. A cultural entrepreneur and bridge between the Odia diaspora and the international film market. Founder of Can-Do Personnel and Majoris Solutions Limited.

Director & Writer · Cinema4Good

Jitendra Mishra

From Titlagarh, Odisha. President of CIJEF, acclaimed director of "I Am Kalam" and "The Last Color." International jury member across 40+ film festivals worldwide, including Cannes.
